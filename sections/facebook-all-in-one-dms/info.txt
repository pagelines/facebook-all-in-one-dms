== Description ==
The Facebook All-In-One section combines three great Facebook Social widgets:
Like Box, Like Button, & Comments. 

Each mode allows you to easily integrate your site with the people on Facebook. 

<h3>Features</h3>
* Let user read recent posts from your Facebook Page
* Enable users to Like your Facebook Page with one click from your website
* Let users see how many already like your Facebook Page, and which of their friends like it too
* Users can share your content with their friends on Facebook 
* Users can comment on your site using their Facebook profile
* Customizable options, size, position, & color scheme
* Custom Open Graph Meta Tags
* No need to copy & paste code
